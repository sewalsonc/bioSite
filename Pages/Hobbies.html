The picture above, Noah is finishing up on a rebuild of 350 cu in Chevy motor. Still picked out what to put it in.

From around the age of 9 he slowly picked it up more and more. Noah had the opportunity to take lessons with Ron Emory of the band TSOL. He became good friends with Ron’s son Jack and this was no doubt an inspiration.
